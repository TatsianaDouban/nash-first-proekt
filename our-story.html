Our story
Our founder, Amy Hutton, woke up one day and realized that there needs to be a change to our food products. Our usual, everyday products tend to be unhealthy for you, bad for the enviorment, and bad for the animals as well. She gathered a group of people and created Fresh Farms. Fresh Farms wants to make a difference in our community and our world as a whole. Our milk is derived from cows that are 100% grass fed. Our jugs are 100% recycable and made of already recycled jugs. If you want a better product and a better world, choose Fresh Farms.
